About Michael Jordan

Jordan was born in Brooklyn, New York, on February 17, 1963, but grew up in Wilmington, North Carolina. He attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he played college basketball and won a national championship in 1982.

Jordan was selected by the Chicago Bulls with the third overall pick in the 1984 NBA Draft. He went on to play for the Bulls for most of his career and also played two seasons with the Washington Wizards.


3/4 of the way into his active career, Jordan left basketball to pursue his first dream as a kid of becoming a baseball player. not long after, he returned to basketball in which he won championships after.

After retiring from basketball, Jordan became a successful businessman and is now the majority owner of the Charlotte Hornets NBA team. His own brand is deemed as one of the most famous brands along with Nike in the world with collaborations reaching other sports such as football being Paris-Saint-Germain's kit creator
